⭐ How to get started... ⭐

Step 1 - Click on the 'Create Room' or 'Get Started' button 😎

Step 2 - Enter a display name in the name box ✍️

Step 3 - Either pick a room name/id or just click 'Go to Room' to be put in a room with a random 🆔

Step 4 - Allow the site to use your microphone 🎙️ and camera 📹

Step 5 - That's it! 🎉 Share your 'Room ID' which will be on the left side of your screen with friends! 🥳
